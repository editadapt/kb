Version: WCAG 2.0+
Number: 2.1.3
Level: AAA
Applicability:
  • Buttons
  • Custom Controls
  • Forms
  • Inputs
Requirement:

All functionality of the content is operable through a keyboard interface without requiring specific timings for individual keystrokes.
Guidance:
  • Understanding Success Criterion 2.1.3
  • How to meet Success Criterion 2.1.3

Purpose

The purpose of this success criterion is to ensure that user that require a keyboard to access a publication have access to all functionality in the content without exception.

How to Meet

The requirements for this success criterion are the same as for 2.4.1 Keyboard.

The only difference is that this success criterion does not allow any exceptions (i.e., any functionality that cannot be replicated by keyboard fails).
