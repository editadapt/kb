Number: 3.1.3
Level: AAA
Applicability:
  • Text
Requirement:

A mechanism is available for identifying specific definitions of words or phrases used in an unusual or restricted way, including idioms and jargon.
Guidance:
  • Understanding Success Criterion 3.1.3
  • How to meet Success Criterion 3.1.3

Purpose

The purpose of this success criterion is to ensure that users with cognitive disabilities can understand specialized terms, including common language used in a specialized way.

How to Meet

Authors should provide a glossary of specialized terms or links to external definitions.
